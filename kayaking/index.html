Kayaking

Kayaking is allowed on the Rio Grande. Visitors are welcome to bring their own kayaks to tour the river.

You can access the river at Anzalduas County Park and Dam or at Chimney Park RV Resort.

There is also a resaca used for kayaking at Bentsen-Rio Grande Valley State Park with a boat ramp.
